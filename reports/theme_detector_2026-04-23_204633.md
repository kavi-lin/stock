## 7. Methodology & Data Quality

### Methodology

The Theme Detector identifies market themes by:

1. **Industry Ranking:** FINVIZ performance data ranked by multi-timeframe composite score (1W 10%, 1M 25%, 3M 35%, 6M 30%)
2. **Theme Classification:** Industries grouped into thematic clusters (AI/Semiconductors, Energy Transition, etc.)
3. **Heat Scoring:** Theme strength measured by performance momentum, volume confirmation, and breadth
4. **Lifecycle Stage:** Theme maturity assessed via Emerging/Accelerating/Trending/Mature/Exhausting classification
5. **Uptrend Overlay:** Monty's sector uptrend ratios provide breadth context

**Note on Confidence:** Script output confidence is capped at Medium. Claude's WebSearch narrative confirmation can elevate confidence to High.

**Note on Direction:** Theme direction (LEAD/LAG) is based on **relative performance of matched industries**, not absolute price change. A LAG theme may still have positive returns — it indicates relative underperformance, not a short signal.
